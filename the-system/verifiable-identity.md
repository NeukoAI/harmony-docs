# Verifiable Identity (Metaplex)

To strengthen trust and partner-facing credibility, Harmony includes a verifiable identity layer through the **Metaplex Agent Registry**. This gives campaign agents public on-chain identity references and structured off-chain service metadata.

In this design:

- **Identity and discoverability** are public and verifiable
- **Campaign execution** remains off-chain for speed, policy control, and human approvals

The separation is intentional — the on-chain layer proves who the agents are, while the off-chain layer handles the operational complexity of running a live campaign.
