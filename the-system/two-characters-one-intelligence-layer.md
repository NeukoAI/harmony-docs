# Two Characters, One Intelligence Layer

The campaign narrative includes two characters. Publicly, participants experience two distinct voices and trajectories. Systemically, those trajectories are coordinated by an agent network that reasons over shared campaign context while preserving lane-specific behavior.

That design enables a strategic surprise later in the arc without requiring retroactive story rewriting. It also allows the campaign to maintain narrative tension while keeping operations coherent behind the scenes.

In practical terms, this is a **dual-perspective narrative with coordinated memory and governance**.
