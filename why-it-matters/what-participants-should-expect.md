# What Participants Should Expect

Participants should expect a campaign that evolves with community behavior, not a fixed script.

- Some mechanics will be visible immediately; others will appear as the story advances
- The methodology is public even when all narrative details are not — **that is intentional**
- As phases complete, we will publish logs and post-experiment analysis documenting both what worked and what failed

---

> _Harmony is an experiment in making campaigns both more adaptive and more trustworthy. If it works, participants experience a story world that responds meaningfully to real behavior and recognizes contribution quality with more clarity. If parts fail, those failures still produce useful boundaries for the next generation of autonomous narrative systems._
>
> _Either way, the result is evidence._
