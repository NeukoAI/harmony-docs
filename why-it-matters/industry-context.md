# Industry Context

Consumer expectations for responsiveness are already high. Sprout Social reports nearly three-quarters of consumers expect responses within 24 hours on social, and 73% would buy from a competitor if brands fail to respond.

Meanwhile, AI capability is moving from novelty to infrastructure. McKinsey estimates generative AI could contribute $2.6 trillion to $4.4 trillion annually in value, with major impact in customer operations and marketing workflows.

**Agentic storytelling tests whether those capabilities can be applied to live culture systems in a way participants can actually feel:** better timing, better attribution, and better trust.
