# What We Are Measuring

The evaluation framework is intentionally plain-language and concrete.

### 1. Adaptation Speed

**Question:** When participant behavior changes, how quickly does the campaign respond?

**Measurement:** Time between a meaningful state shift and a narrative or operational adjustment.

**Success condition:** Adjustments happen within the active cycle window, not after momentum is gone.

### 2. Relationship Quality

**Question:** Does the system identify participants who create durable positive network effects?

**Measurement:** Repeat contribution quality, bridge behavior across groups, and downstream action quality from referred or influenced participants.

**Success condition:** High-trust contributors are recognized consistently, not only high-volume actors.

### 3. Decision Fairness and Reliability

**Question:** Do verification and reward decisions remain consistent under load?

**Measurement:** Review latency, reversal rate, exception handling quality, and evidence completeness.

**Success condition:** Faster throughput with stable standards and low dispute rates.

### 4. Narrative Continuity

**Question:** Does the story feel coherent as it adapts?

**Measurement:** Phase-to-phase retention, return participation, and continuity of participant understanding.

**Success condition:** Adaptation increases momentum without making the narrative feel random or fragmented.

### 5. Participation Experience

**Question:** Do participants feel seen and clearly oriented in the system?

**Measurement:** Qualitative sentiment, confusion rate around mechanics, and clarity of progression signals.

**Success condition:** Participants report stronger clarity and agency over the campaign arc.
